A life lesson you may need to hear!

Posted :  6/16/2010 5:02:24 PM  |

 0 Comments   

By  

  

my ear time and time again “talk about people as if you were talking with their sister or brother”. Little did I understand the profound advice I was receiving as a child and I didn’t really get what she meant until many years later, but fortunately I’ve followed this advice my entire life (of course with a slip up here and there).

 

I guess what I’m getting at is if you are saying something that couldn’t be said to someone’s face, why say it at all? I mean really, what would be the benefit of talking trash about someone when they can’t even defend themselves? I know I know, sometimes it feel so good to vent, right? Think about this, what would happen if the person that was the recipient of the trash talking was able to hear you; to hear what you though of them? Do you think they would laugh it off? Or be able to walk away with their head held high? I’ll tell you one thing, I don’t know many people with such thick skin that they wouldn’t be hurt or upset by negative words or crude remarks by another person. Every time I watch someone get “busted” talking trash, it’s not the sender that is usually hurt. I can literally see the sadness in the eyes of the person who hears what you really think about them.

 

If you’re guilty of doing this sometimes, this isn’t to make you feel bad or for you to go apologize today. It’s just a simple life lesson reminder from my mom that I wanted to share with each of you.

 

Remember, “Talk about others as if you were talking to their sister or brother” and you will know that you’re taking the high road in life. There is nothing better than knowing that you did the right thing and followed your beliefs and values in a tough situation. Trust yourself to be the person you’ve always wanted to be.

 

You are my inspiration!
