A Lesson for my Mac

Posted :  6/16/2010 3:32:00 PM  |

 0 Comments   

By  System Administrator

  

Last year I finally took the plunge, I switched from a PC to a Mac. I’ve got to say the first few months were tough… trying to figure out how to copy, save, and find files. Heck, even shutting the computer down was a bit different. I started to think I had made a mistake in the first week. I thought to myself, “Am I ever going to get this?’.

Finally one day it clicked, I figured out that the Mac platform is a lot easier if you just do what makes sense in your own mind. Meaning if I want a photo in a specific area, I just drag it there. Or if I want to find a file, guess where I go… um, yeah… the finder.

Why do I tell you this? George Leonard said it best in his book Mastery; he said “Mastery is the mysterious process during which what is at first difficult becomes progressively easier and more pleasurable through practice”. So if you want to enjoy a task or challenge more, you’ll need to practice more before it becomes more pleasurable.

So often, we find ourselves giving up on a goal before we attain it. Or beginning a new habit, just to break it or quit before the finish line.

What is one goal you have, that if you achieved it, would undoubtedly change your life?

Take a moment to write down your goal… only one for today.

Now, write down everything that you can think of, every area of your life that will positively change by your accomplishing your goal.

Next, write down everyone’s name you can think of that will be impacted in a positive way when you achieve your goal. Remember, even if your goal is to lose weight, more than just you will be affected. Maybe you can play with your children more or live longer so you can travel the world with your partner.

Be clear, your life’s actions, positive or negative, affect the lives of many people around you. Positive changes in your life, will ultimately affect tens, if not hundreds of lives in the long run.

Don’t let it go to waste. Live your Ideal life!

I’m honored to be part of your journey.

Your Ideal Life coach,

Josh
