Reinvent yourself

Posted :  6/16/2010 3:45:31 PM  |

 0 Comments   

By  System Administrator

  

Have you ever dreamt that you could totally reinvent yourself? Or have you ever wished deep down that you had what it takes to be more successful?

Many times, people go through life as if their success… or lack there of, is part of their DNA. They figure that somehow, someway their parents passed a unique trait down that will either help them achieve success or prevent them from ever being successful. The reality is that although we may receive good or bad traits from our parents, WE ultimately control the outcome in our lives.

I’ll give you an example – I want you to imagine a little girl that was born into poverty in rural Mississippi to a teenage single mother. At a very young age, she had to leave her mother and move in with her grandparents; which was in a very poor environment.. At six, she moved back in with her mother who lived in a small farming town in Mississippi where the little girl was constantly sexually abused by numerous male relatives and also by friends of her mother. At 13 years old, she was sent to live with her father in Tennessee where she struggled with drugs. At 14, she became pregnant; shortly thereafter her son died in infancy. Imagine someone living through all of these struggles before the age of 16. How many of us would just give up? Who would say “oh well, this is the life I was born in to” or “the life I deserve”?

Many of us probably have our own opinion of where this little girl should’ve ended up; maybe on the streets, a drug addict, prostitute or maybe even dead before the age of 20. The truth is that this little girl is the perfect example of one’s “story” or “past” not foretelling their future. She could have chosen to let what happened to her hinder the rest of her life… but she didn’t.

Who is this little girl you may ask? Many of you already know or had guessed that this is the childhood of Talk Shot Host and Television Mogul Oprah Winfrey; who overcame such a tough childhood to become quite possibly the most influential woman in the world. Pretty amazing, don’t you agree?

When asked about her tremendous success coming from such a troubled background she said – “I knew there was a way out. I knew there was another kind of life because I had read about it. I knew there were other places, and there was another way of being.”

Now… you’ve seen what else is out there waiting for you, haven’t you? There is another kind of life, a life that is ideal for YOU. You must embrace it and go after it. You must make the decision that you deserve more. We all have had challenges in our lives. We’ve all made poor decisions at one time or another. We’ve all been hurt. You’re not alone.

But where did you get the idea that that meant you should have a “less than” life? At what point did you decide to settle?

Look, now is the perfect time to change. It’s the new year of 2010… 2009 is over! More importantly, so is 2008, 2007, 2006 and so on. Now is the time to figure out what you truly want and “GO FOR IT”!

With all of this said, If you don’t know how… or don’t know what you want, I’m here for you. If you’ve ever needed a business partner to help you thrive in business, I’m here for you. If you need a sounding board to keep you on track, I’m here for you. If you’re ready to say screw it, I’ll give it a shot, I’ll be standing next to you watching you give it your all. You just call and I’ll be there.

I wish you the best of everything not only in 2010, but in every moment of your journey.
