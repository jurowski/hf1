What are Impact Points?

You influence the world around you by what you do and by what you choose not to do.
Impact Points are a way to measure that, at least for this little corner of our lives.

How can I earn Impact Points?

1. Send a message to someone else, and you earn points for the little bit of influence that you're sending their way.
2. Check in with a "yes", and you'll earn points too, because each success you have affects those around you in subtle but important ways.

What can I do with my Impact Points?

Feel Good and Smile, knowing you're sending good vibes out to the world, changing it for the better one little piece at a time.
