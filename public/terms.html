What can I expect from the HabitForge application?

We will work to keep you on track with any habit you're trying to make or break. We will do this through the web application interface, and through email check-ins in which we ask you if you were successful that day, and from which you can click "yes" or "no". We'll be sending emails that you can "turn off" at any time. If you do not "check in" for more than two weeks, we will assume that you want to put that habit "on hold" and we will stop sending the check-ins.

Is there a Community? Will they support me as well?

If the habit that you're working on is set to "public", others in the HabitForge community may contact you to encourage your progress in forming that habit. With a Premium Membership, you have the option of "private habits" in which the habit is not visible to the HabitForge community.
