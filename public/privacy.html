HabitForge Privacy Policy and Terms of Use

We will never sell your email address or share it with a third party. By signing up with HabitForge, you agree to be reached by email so that you can report on your goal progress. You can also receive goal reminders if you enable them. Additionally, HabitForge may send habit-related newsletter content, which you can unsubscribe from at any time via an unsubscribe link in the email. If you do choose to share goals with other members of this site, only your first name will be shown. While members can send messages to one another, email addresses will never be exposed (all member to member email will be routed through HabitForge addresses).
